Activity¶

Fully qualified class name: DDICDIModels::Context::PROV::Activity

Definition¶

Something that occurs over a period of time and acts upon or with entities; it may include consuming, processing, transforming, modifying, relocating, using, or generating entities. (See the W3C PROV-O ontology, https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-dm/#term-Activity)
