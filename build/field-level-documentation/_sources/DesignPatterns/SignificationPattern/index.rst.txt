SignificationPattern
********************

| **Fully qualified package name**: DDICDIModels::DesignPatterns::SignificationPattern

Template classes describing the symbolization of concepts or objects, and are related to the way in which meanings are represented in the model. This covers only the representation of individual units of meaning - the relationships between and among them are described in the Collections Pattern.
